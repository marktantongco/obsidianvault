# Free China Proxy Research (source)

> Source: `Downloads/@ai/free-china-proxy-research.md` (2026-05-20)

## What It Covers
Free vs paid China proxy landscape for OrcaFlow-style China AI API access.

## Key Points
- Free CN proxies scarce; many stale or SOCKS-only.
- Best free lists: Databay, Spys.one (scrape), ProxyScrape CN when non-empty.
- Expect 20–40% success; refresh hourly.
- Prefer direct; only proxy blocked providers; never global HTTP_PROXY.
